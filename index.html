Hourglass

Win the race against time.

Get where you need to be on time

Hourglass gives you a slight vibration to let you know that you should leave in a few minutes to get to your next event. Using your calendar and GPS coordinates, Hourglass knows how long it will take for you to walk, take public transit, or drive to where you need to go.

Maps + directions when you need them

Many times you don't need directions for where you're going. But, when you do, swipe up for a list of directions or down for a map. Otherwise, a simple progress bar will let you know how much longer to go.

Know your friends' ETAs

Not sure when your friends are going to meet you at the movie theatre? Simply swipe left to see when they'll be arriving regardless of whether they have an iOS or Android device. Tap the "On My Way!" button to let them know you've headed out.

Map the world

We know it's not easy to find Conference Room C or that hip yoga store in the mall. That's why Hourglass users are helping map the world one step at a time. When you find yourself shopping for shoes or walking to your next meeting, pat yourself on the back! You're helping create directories for other Hourglass users to find exactly where they're looking to go.
